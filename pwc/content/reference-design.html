About our San Diego Psychiatric Wellness Center

Psychiatric Wellness Center is dedicated to providing expert psychiatric care through a team of highly trained professionals, including physicians who have received their training at prestigious institutions such as UCLA and USC. We understand that mental health is a deeply personal journey, which is why our Psychiatrists take a compassionate, patient-centered approach to treatment. By actively listening and creating individualized care plans, we ensure that every patient receives the tailored support they need to heal, grow, and thrive.

With years of experience, Psychiatric Wellness Center has earned a well-respected reputation across California for offering comprehensive and effective mental health services. Our commitment to enhancing the well-being of individuals has positively impacted countless lives, and now, we are proud to extend our trusted services to North County San Diego, a convenient location for Encinitas, Carlsbad, San Marcos, and Escondido residents. Whether you’re seeking therapy, medication management, or other psychiatric services, we are here to help you on your path to mental wellness with the same level of care and excellence that has made us a leader in the field.

Meet Dr. Kharkar

Your Partner in Mental Wellness

We’re excited to introduce Dr. Kharkar, a UCLA-trained, board-certified psychiatrist with over 10 years of experience, now serving North County San Diego at the Psychiatric Wellness Center. Dr. Kharkar holds a degree in Neuroscience from UCLA and a medical degree (MD) from USC Keck School of Medicine, with advanced training in psychiatry at UCLA-SFV, where she served as Chief Inpatient Resident. Her approach integrates psychopharmacology, psychotherapy, and holistic care to treat the whole person, taking into account genetics, medical conditions, and life experiences.

At the Psychiatric Wellness Center, Dr. Kharkar is dedicated to providing personalized care tailored to each individual’s unique needs. She listens carefully to understand your mental health concerns and collaborates with you to create a comprehensive treatment plan that promotes lasting well-being. As a new resource in the area, the center is committed to empowering patients to achieve a balanced and fulfilling life.

Depression

Medication Assisted Treatment

Medication assisted treatment is often a vital step for individuals facing substance use disorders, especially when cravings, withdrawal symptoms and physical dependence make it feel impossible to stop on your own. We understand how challenging this struggle can be and this treatment combines FDA-approved medications with therapy to provide the support you need to heal, reduce cravings and prevent relapse, helping you regain control of your life and move toward lasting recovery.

At the Psychiatric Wellness Center, our team of experienced psychiatrists, conveniently located in North County San Diego, is here to provide the support and care you need on your path to lasting recovery.

Our Psychiatrists serve Carlsbad, San Marcos, Escondido and the greater San Diego area.

Postpartum Depression

Postpartum depression is a challenging and often overwhelming experience that can bring feelings of sadness, anxiety and exhaustion, making it difficult to care for both yourself and your newborn. It’s important to remember that you are not alone, and with the right support, healing is possible. 

At the Psychiatric Wellness Center, our compassionate team of psychiatrists in North County San Diego is here to offer the understanding and care you need to navigate this difficult time and help you find your way to recovery.

Our Psychiatrists serve Carlsbad, San Marcos, Escondido and the greater San Diego area.

Adult ADHD

Psychiatric Wellness Center’s Psychiatrists in Encinitas, CA, conduct a thorough evaluation for adult ADHD that includes clinical interviews, symptom checklists and a review of symptom history, while also ruling out other potential causes such as anxiety or depression.

Panic Disorder

Panic disorder is marked by sudden, intense fear episodes called panic attacks, which can cause physical symptoms that impact mental health. Those who frequently experience symptoms, worry about future panic attacks or find them affecting daily life, should contact Psychiatric Wellness Center for evaluation and treatment.

Our Psychiatrists serve Carlsbad, San Marcos, Escondido and the greater San Diego area.

Alcohol Use Disorder

Alcohol use disorder is a condition where individuals struggle to control their alcohol consumption, often leading to negative effects on their health, relationships and daily life. If you find yourself frequently craving alcohol, drinking more than you intend, or facing challenges in cutting back despite the consequences, it’s important to know that help is available.

At the Psychiatric Wellness Center, you will receive the compassionate, personalized care you deserve to overcome alcohol use disorder and support your journey toward lasting recovery.

Our Psychiatrists serve Carlsbad, San Marcos, Escondido and the greater San Diego area.

Women's Health

At Psychiatric Wellness Center, we offer personalized care through therapy, medication management, and holistic strategies to help women achieve emotional balance and overall well-being.
Our Psychiatrists serve Carlsbad, San Marcos, Escondido and the greater San Diego area.

Professional Burnout

Professional burnout can have a profound impact on mental health, leading to chronic stress, anxiety, depression, and a diminished sense of purpose, all of which can negatively affect both personal well-being and professional performance. Our Professional Burnout Services are focused on helping individuals navigate the emotional and physical exhaustion caused by chronic workplace stress and dissatisfaction.
Through personalized therapy, stress management techniques, and support for work-life balance, we empower clients to recover from burnout and regain a sense of purpose and well-being in both their professional and personal lives.
Our Psychiatrists serve Carlsbad, San Marcos, Escondido and the greater San Diego area.
