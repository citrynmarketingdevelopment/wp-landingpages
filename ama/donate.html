What impact does my gift make?

Every level of giving builds hope

Concrete examples of how your gift comes alongside couples in our community who are struggling with infertility.

$25

Comfort Giver

Helps place a comfort kit into the hands of a couple walking through loss or treatment — a tangible reminder that they are loved and not alone.

$75

Prayer Partner

Sustains our prayer team and support group gatherings, giving couples a safe, faith-filled place to share their journey and be encouraged.

$125

Hope Builder

Fuels community events and outreach that break the stigma surrounding infertility and connect more families to the resources they need.

$250

Angel Partner

Goes directly toward AMA Family Grants — easing the financial burden of IVF, adoption, and surrogacy and helping make someone’s dream of family come true.

Why give monthly?

Steady hands make lasting change

Lasting Impact

Reliable monthly support lets us plan family grants and programs with confidence all year long — so help is always there the moment a family needs it.

Convenience

It’s quick and easy to set up your recurring gift online — then it takes care of itself every month, and you can update or pause it any time.

Community

Angels of Hope givers become part of the AMA family — celebrating every grant awarded, every comfort kit delivered, and every miracle baby along the way.
