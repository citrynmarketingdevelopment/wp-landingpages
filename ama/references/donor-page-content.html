Renovare: Music for the Whole
Give a Gift Donate Now Give Monthly Heartstrings Volunteer
 
 
 

Make a consistent impact as part of Heartstrings, our monthly giving community, and receive exclusive video updates about the impact you’re making - and more!

Renovare’s monthly givers allow us to confidently plan long-term, impactful projects 12 months every year. These faithful partners sustain the work of touching hearts and minds through music. 

What impact does my gift make?

This chart gives concrete examples of how you can love your neighbor, affirm the value of all people, and cultivate reconciled communities through your monthly donation.

 
Featured
Melody Maker ~ $25

Allows one person in a hospital, prison, or homeless shelter to experience a Renovare concert, allowing them to hear beautiful music and be reminded of their God-given value.
Ensemble Enthusiast ~ $75

Pays for a group of people in prison to have an hour of music instruction led by a skilled teaching artist, allowing them to grow in self-esteem and build community, as they express themselves through music.
Harmony Builder ~ $125

Pays for one Teaching Artist to co-lead a Documentary Songwriting session, offering a member of the community the therapeutic and empowering experience of sharing their story through song.
Virtuoso Partner ~ $250

Provides 4 performances each year in a hospital, prison, or homeless shelter, allowing an entire room full of people to experience the restorative power of music and relationship.

 

Why give monthly?

  • Special Access

    You’ll receive a welcome packet when you sign up, plus periodic emails with links to access sneak peeks of projects, video updates, and more via the Heartstrings member-only page on our website. 

  • Convenience

    It’s quick and easy to set up your recurring gift online, then it will take care of itself every month.

  • Community

    Monthly givers have opportunities to meet one another and be encouraged in their lives and faith by others in the Heartstrings community.
